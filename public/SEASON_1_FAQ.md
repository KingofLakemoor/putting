# Putting World Season 1 FAQs

## Cost
$15 for fun to play, $20 for competition play. All payments should be made through Club602.com.

## Format
Tour format. Competitors will play 2 Rounds each night, with their cumulative score being used for end of night rankings. Competitors who do not finish two rounds will be removed from the standings.

## Prizes
Prizes will be determined by the overall prize pot. 60% of the pot each night will pay to first place.

## Open Play
Open Play is encouraged throughout the night. Happy Hour pricing is in effect all night long.
